About me

At a very Young age of my life i was blessed with the Study of Yoga. I began Growing and entered my Teens and build a hobby of reading ancient texts and novels. Since then there was no looking back . That was when the seeds of Spirituality and Yoga began growing in me and I kept growing inside that. There was no end to this thirst. I wanted to know more, explore more. The second turning point in my Life happened when I got my admission in University of Patanjali for my Masters in Yoga Science. That's when I realised "this is it". Though there were times I was lost and misguided but every time I landed at the same point. No matter how hard I tried to drift away from this I just could not. I tried new different things just to test myself and move away from Yoga but I just could not. Nothing gave me peace or contentment. And then I finally decided to call it all quits and pursue my passion. (Walking the path and exploring more about Yoga).
